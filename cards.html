Card block

The Card block is an all-around block to display content in a clean layout. Use this block to elegantly display information and photos to showcase your portfolio, team photos, services, and more
With Card blocks, you can:

  • Showcase your services and allow people to learn more about them
  • Enumerate your products
  • Display content in an organized card layout
  • Highlight your services
